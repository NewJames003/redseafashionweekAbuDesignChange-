Discover the Essence of

Red Sea Fashion Week

Red Sea Fashion Week is not just an event; it's a cultural phenomenon that bridges the past and the future of fashion in Saudi Arabia. It serves as a platform to amplify the voices of local talent while inviting international designers to explore the richness of Saudi culture through contemporary fashion narratives.

Our Mission


At the fashion commission, our mission is to nurture a thriving fashion industry that is sustainable, inclusive, and fully integrated along the value chain. We are dedicated to maximizing local talent and competencies through our strategic objectives which span education, product development, manufacturing, retail, and sustainability.

A Legacy of Fashion

Previous editions of our fashion events, including the Riyadh Fashion Week, have set precedents in the industry, showcasing the transformative power of fashion and its ability to convey cultural stories and identities.
